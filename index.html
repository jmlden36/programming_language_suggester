Programming Language Suggester

Welcome to the Programming Language Suggester. If you are here then you are probably curious about joining the ever growing field of software development. But where should you start? We believe that a good first step is to get an idea of what programming languages you might be interested in. That's where the Programming Language Suggester comes in. We will ask you a series of questions, and based on your answers, we can help steer you toward the programming language of your dreams! Please answer your questions on a 1 to 3 scale where 1 indicates you strongly agree, 2 indicates you are indifferent, and 3 indicates you strongly disagree.
